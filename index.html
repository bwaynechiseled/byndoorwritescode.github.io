About Me

I am Shreyas Byndoor, a normal dude in his early twenties chasing excitement and trying to inspire others. I created this website to share the stuff I work on, write about topics that I find insightful, and also share high protein vegetarian recipes. I currently work as systems software engineer in the silicon industry for my day job. Writing, creating videos, sports / fitness and tech projects arouse my curiosity and this website will be a one stop for my creative endeavors. I have been in a continuous pursuit of lifestyle design and self-improvement and I want this website to also be a platform where I can document my progress.
